Research Interests

I am currently learning how to use the NEMESIS code for atmospheric retrieval of exoplanets.

Exo-Venuses

My primary interest is in understanding the atmospheres of potential Venus-like exoplanets, otherwise known and Exo-Venuses.

JWST ERS Transit: WASP 39 b

I was a member the JWST ERS Transit Program. A part of the research conducted by this collaboration led to the first confirmation of atmospheric CO2 in the atmosphere of WASP 39 b using JWST.
